Martial Arts

Martial arts is more than just fighting — it's a way of life. It teaches discipline, confidence, respect, and resilience. Every time you step onto the mat, you grow stronger — not just physically, but mentally. You learn how to face challenges, how to push through fear, and how to keep going when things get tough.

Private Tuition

Our private tuition sessions are designed to meet your unique goals — whether you're preparing for a grading, sharpening specific techniques, or just want focused attention. Work one-on-one with our skilled coaches to fast-track your progress in any of the martial arts we offer. These flexible, personalized lessons are suitable for all ages and experience levels.

Judo

Judo is a dynamic martial art focused on throws, trips, joint locks, and pins. At Tiger Martial Arts, we emphasize technique, timing, and balance. Classes include drills, sparring (randori), and groundwork, all taught by qualified instructors. Judo enhances coordination, agility, and mental strategy, making it great for self-defense and competitive sport.

Karate

Our Karate program blends traditional values with modern training. Students learn precise techniques, katas (forms), and sparring to develop physical and mental discipline. Karate builds confidence, self-control, and focus, making it ideal for both children and adults. We promote a positive and respectful environment where each student can grow and succeed at their own pace.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai is Thailand’s national sport and one of the most effective striking arts in the world. Our classes teach the fundamentals of punching, kicking, elbow, and knee strikes, combined with clinch work and footwork drills. You'll gain cardiovascular fitness, mental toughness, and real-world self-defense skills, all while training in a high-energy, motivating atmosphere. Perfect for beginners and fighters alike.

Jiu-Jitsu

Jiu-Jitsu is a powerful martial art that focuses on leverage, technique, and ground control rather than brute strength. At Tiger Martial Arts, our Jiu-Jitsu program teaches both Gi and No-Gi techniques, covering takedowns, submissions, escapes, and positional control. Whether you're training for self-defense, competition, or personal growth, our structured classes provide a supportive and challenging environment for all levels.
